Leevy is a five piece band based in West Cork.

The music of Leevy is a unique blend of folk rock poetry, raucous punk delivery with Irish traditional undertones.

Press Release for the New Album:


Title: 'Baile Mhúirne or The Soldiers March the Paps of Anú'


“There is rhythm in the sadness; there is rhythm in the sod”

Grown from the boggy outcrop of the Muskerry Gaeltacht, Leevy are finding beauty and madness in the harshest corners of the Irish psyche.

Leevy’s debut album ‘Baile Mhúirne or The Soldiers March the Paps of Anú’ encapsulates the songs and sounds the band have honed through relentless gigging in recent years; from tales of sacrificial burnings at wakes, to forlorn songs of loss and the death of the natural world, Leevy’s debut release dances around the threshold of this world and the next.

With a sound that can roar with pounding drums, banjos, whistles, and heavy guitars or can just as easily sway with beautiful folk arrangements and harmonies that tie their raucous performance to tradition and culture.

The 13 track album will be available on Vinyl and CD from May 2026. Tour Dates below.
