LATEST MUSIC VIDEO

Burn the Casket

Folk Punk band Leevy’s latest single “Burn the Casket”, from their upcoming debut album, is a rural punk ballad, full of manic energy and lyrical grotesquery.

It’s built on a driving rhythm section, gritty electric guitars, and frantic mandolin and whistles that sound like they were pulled straight out of an other worldly céilí. Fast and loud, but the story it’s telling is unsettling and deranged.
