Leevy.

Leevy is a five-piece band from the Múscraí Gaeltacht in West Cork, Ireland. The band is led by singer songwriter Amhlaoibh McSweeney.

LATEST EP

Very Sad Lives

Fresh new Irish music from Leevy.

Leevy’s latest release, Very Sad Lives is a six track EP that builds on the previous releases of the group, showcasing a strong storytelling capability in the lyrics (in keeping with the Irish tradition) supported by a catchy and complex set of melodies and compositions that celebrate the band’s roots in traditional music, punk and country.
