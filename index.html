Leevy is the sound of a generation.

Leevy is a five-piece band from the Múscraí Gaeltacht in West Cork. The band is led by singer songwriter Amhlaoibh McSweeney (influences: Neil Young / Tom Waits).

NEW EP

Very Sad Lives

Fresh new Irish music from Leevy.

Leevy’s latest release, Very Sad Lives is a six track EP that builds on the previous releases of the group, showcasing a strong storytelling capability in the lyrics (in keeping with the Irish tradition) supported by a catchy and complex set of melodies and compositions that celebrate the band’s roots in traditional music (Sweeney’s Men / The Pogues), punk (Pixies) and country (Creedence Clearwater Revival).
